PRD — AI-Assisted Sales Recommendation Feature

Document type: Sample PRD (anonymised)

Product: Internal Sales Quotation Platform

Audience: Engineering, Sales, Stakeholders

1. Overview

Sales teams assemble tailored golf travel packages by combining travel dates, golf course and tee-time availability, accommodation, and transportation for both domestic and international travellers. The product supports inbound and outbound golf tours, golf breaks, and holiday packages.

An AI-assisted recommendation capability suggests the most suitable package combinations based on group size, budget, destination, dates, and preferences. This improves consistency and significantly reduces quote turnaround time.

2. Problem Statement

Pricing and availability are sourced from disconnected channels such as PDFs, spreadsheets, websites, and emails. Sales teams manually reconcile this data, making the process slow and error-prone.

Changes in customer requirements lead to repeated quote revisions, further extending turnaround time and delaying conversion.

3. Goal

Enable sales teams to generate automated total pricing and receive data-driven recommendations to accelerate conversion.

4. Success Metrics

  • Reduction in average time to generate a quote
  • Adoption rate of AI recommendations
  • Reduction in number of quote revisions

5. Users

  • Primary: Sales Executives
  • Secondary: Account Managers

6. In Scope

  • Capture structured customer requirements
  • Generate automated itinerary pricing
  • Rank recommendations based on suitability

7. Out of Scope

  • Fully automated decision-making
  • Customer-facing recommendation flows
  • Payment or booking confirmation

8. Assumptions & Risks

Assumptions

  • Pricing data is accessible from source systems
  • Availability can be reliably retrieved

Risks

  • Over-reliance on recommendations
  • Data quality issues
  • Availability mismatches

9. Acceptance Criteria

  • Recommendations align with inputs and business rules
  • Pricing is accurately calculated
  • System responses meet performance thresholds
